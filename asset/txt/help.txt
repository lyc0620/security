HELP - SYSTEM COMMANDS
==============================
> log [user]
    Checks the log of the user.
    Only Effective for offline user.

> ping [user]
    Scans the response latency.
    Use with caution.
    They might notice.

> msg [user]
    Send the message to the other user.
    Use with caution.
    It can directly connect to them.
